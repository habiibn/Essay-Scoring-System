About Program

The exam is an assessment tool in the teaching and learning process to determine the quality of learning. The most common methods in the exam are multiple choice and essays. Multiple choice answers can be easily corrected because they only need to match the available answer keys, but they are different from essay answers. Correcting essay answers is a problem in the world of education because a lot of time used by educators is wasted to correct content that is actually relatively the same. From these problems, our team offers a solution in the form of a web-based essay assessment application entitled EasyMe.
